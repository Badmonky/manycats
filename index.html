What is TimeWriter?

A social experiment in collaborative writing of an infinite story expanding forever into the past and the future.

To ensure that a creative and valuable story will develop we will utilize NFT auctions as submissions for story line snippets (Days). This way there is a potential cost associated to submissions which ensures nobody submits bullshit. Furthermore to ensure that always the best next piece is added to the story, we will vote on all submissions as a DAO. Each NFT holder will have a vote in the DAO, and if one wins the auction (through being voted the best submission), they will be the holder of that newly created NFT and can then vote on the continuation of the story.
